## FAQ (5 Real Q&As)

### Q1: Is this actually free?
**A:** Yes. Completely free, no credit card required. We make money if you save money and buy discounted credits through Credex. No hidden fees.

### Q2: Why should I trust your numbers?
**A:** Every price point comes from official vendor pricing pages, verified and dated. The audit logic is deterministic (not AI guessing)—it checks: Are you on the right plan for your team size? Is there a cheaper plan that covers your needs? Are you eligible for bulk discounts? All reasoning is shown in your results.

### Q3: Will the audit work for my exact situation?
**A:** The audit covers 8 major tools (Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, Windsurf, Anthropic API, OpenAI API). If you use all of these, it's extremely accurate. If you use something else, you can still input it as "API spend" and the audit will flag cost reduction opportunities relative to industry alternatives.

### Q4: What happens to my email after I capture the report?
**A:** Your email is stored securely in our database. Credex will reach out only if your audit shows >$500/month in savings (high-intent leads). Otherwise, we'll notify you if new optimizations apply to your stack. You can unsubscribe anytime.

### Q5: Can I share my audit results with my team?
**A:** Yes—every audit gets a public link. Your identifying info (email, company name) is stripped from the shared version. You'll see your AI tools and total savings, and your team can see the full reasoning behind each recommendation. It's shareable on Slack, Twitter, email, etc.

## Why This Copy Works

- **Headline is benefit-focused, not feature-focused.** "Find hidden savings" resonates; "AI spend auditor" doesn't.
- **Subheadline removes friction.** "2 minutes, no signup" are the exact objections users have.
- **Social proof is honest.** Real dollar amounts and actual use cases (CTOs, VPs).
- **CTA is verb-driven.** "Audit my AI spend" is more compelling than "Get started" (everyone says that).
- **FAQ pre-empts doubts.** Trust, pricing accuracy, coverage, privacy, sharing—all answered upfront.
